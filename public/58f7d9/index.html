Coaching at Cricket Revolution

Book your lanes, add your athletes to each session, and manage your roster — all from your phone.

Getting in & out at the facility

Getting in

Use the silver Sebury keypad (not the black intercom). Enter your door code, then press # — the roller door opens, then closes automatically on a 30-second timer once the entrance is clear.

Getting out

Press the green “PRESS TO OPEN DOOR” button on the wall — the roller door opens to let you out, then closes again on a 30-second timer once the entrance is clear.

Your door code is in your booking confirmation. Full facility instructions & house rules: cricketrevolution.com.au/facility-instructions
This guide is for coaches. You book lanes at your coach rate (no online payment — settled on your statement), then allocate your athletes to each session so they get their own door code and reminders. Athletes/parents must pick you as their coach (or you add them) before they appear in your lists.

1. Booking a lane

Open Book a Net, pick your day, then tap an open cell on the lane you want. As a coach you book on whole hours (plus the 3:30pm weekday after-school slot). There's no online payment — your sessions are billed to your statement.

You can book any open slot within your booking window; newly released slots open Sunday 5pm.

2. Adding athletes to a session

This is the key step. Open a booking (from the confirmation, or tap the session on My Bookings) to open the Athlete Allocations editor. The app pre-fills back-to-back slots based on your default session length and how many athletes you coach at once — you just drop a name into each slot.

  1. Tap a slot's “Select an athlete…” and pick from your list (type to search).
  2. Adjust each athlete's start time and length (30 / 45 / 60 / 75 / 90 min) — the timeline shows gaps and overlaps.
  3. Need someone not in your list yet? Tap ＋ Add athlete (see §3).
  4. Tap Save. Each allocated athlete (or their parent) gets the door code and a session reminder.
Sequential vs at-once. By default slots run back-to-back (one after another). If your profile is set to coach several at once, slots can overlap in the same lane. Set this on your profile / ask admin.

3. Your athlete roster (add & invite)

Your athletes come from two places: athletes (or parents) who pick you as their coach when they sign up, and ones you add yourself. Manage them under Profile → My Athletes, or add on the fly from the allocation editor.

Adding someone

  • Already have an account? Enter their parent email + athlete name — they're added to your list straight away (“Added {name} — they're now in your list.”).
  • No account yet? The same form sends them an invite (“Invite sent to {email}.”). When they register they're auto-linked to you.
A parent account can have several child athletes. You see the athlete's name and can allocate them; the parent manages the login, contact details and any payment.

4. Your schedule & coverage dots

My Bookings shows your week as a date strip with a coloured dot under each day, and the selected day's sessions below. Toggle Week / List at the top-right (List shows everything upcoming).

The dot tells you, at a glance, how well a day is allocated:

  • Red — a session that day has no athletes yet.
  • Amber — there's an unallocated gap of 30 min or more.
  • Green — fully allocated.

On each session card, your allocated time shows in your coach colour with athlete names; unallocated time shows as an amber-striped gap. Tap a gap to allocate it; tap a name to edit that slot.

🔒 Managed by admin: if admin created the booking for you, you can still allocate athletes, but Modify / Cancel / Repeat are hidden — contact admin to change the booking itself.

5. Repeating a session

Tap ↻ Repeat on a session to book the same slot next week — same lane, time and athlete allocations. A preview shows exactly what will be booked. Repeat unlocks once next week is in your booking window.

6. Changing or cancelling

From a session card, ✏️ Modify lets you change lane, date, time or length; ✕ Cancel cancels it. Coaches have no late-cancel lock, but cancelling within the admin-set window is still charged to your statement.

  • Keep-what-fits: if you shorten or move a session, athletes who still fit are kept; any dropped get a “session changed/removed” email automatically.
  • Allocated athletes are always notified of a reschedule or cancellation (with the new time + door code).

7. Statements & earnings

Open Statements (in the menu) to see your sessions and payments reconciled. Every coach booking is charged automatically at your session price; admin enters your payments and any adjustments. The Outstanding balance is booked − paid.

8. What athletes & parents get told

When you allocate or change a session, the app emails the athlete's parent account (addressed to the child). These are automatic and mandatory, and siblings are combined into one email per family.
