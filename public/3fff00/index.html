Booking at Cricket Revolution

Sign up, book a net, pay, get your door code, and manage it all from your phone.

Getting in & out at the facility

Getting in

Use the silver Sebury keypad (not the black intercom). Enter your door code, then press # — the roller door opens, then closes automatically on a 30-second timer once the entrance is clear.

Getting out

Press the green “PRESS TO OPEN DOOR” button on the wall — the roller door opens to let you out, then closes again on a 30-second timer once the entrance is clear.

Your door code is in your booking confirmation (email + on screen). Full facility instructions & house rules: cricketrevolution.com.au/access

House rules & at the facility

Your lane

The 5 lanes are numbered inside the building — your booking confirmation tells you which one. Lighting is automatic, and bowling-machine operating instructions are on the machine.

House rules

  • Footwear & surface: clean, flat-soled, non-marking shoes only — no spikes or metal cleats in the building. No food or chewing gum in the nets.
  • Safety: protective gear (helmet, pads, gloves, box) is compulsory. One batter in the lane at a time; everyone else outside the net. Only one person operating the machine. Never reach into the machine while it's on. Anyone under 18 must be supervised. Maximum 3 people per lane.
  • Equipment & care: operate machines only as instructed. Collect all balls and leave the crate on the machine stand. Do NOT mix crates of balls — anyone found mixing crates will be banned. Report any fault via the app.
  • Conduct: keep noise down — we back onto homes and a bowling club. No alcohol, smoking or vaping anywhere on site.
  • Leaving: take all your belongings and rubbish. The lights switch off and the roller door closes automatically.
Urgent, or can't get in (any hour): call Julian on 0451 016 151. Emergency exits are on the West wall by the run-up nets and the main entrance door — in a fire, get out and call 000. Full facility instructions: cricketrevolution.com.au/access

Open Book a Net, pick your day, then tap an open ＋ cell on the lane and time you want. There are 5 lanes — Bowling Machine 1–3 and 9m Run Up 1–2 (Bowling Machine 3 also offers the premium Truman machine).

  • ＋ = open · Booked = taken · a 30 min cell is a short gap you can grab · a full hour shows JOIN WAITLIST.
  • In the booking box: pick the machine type and length, see the price, add a discount code or your account credit, then pay securely by card. You get your door code straight away.

3. Your bookings — change or cancel

Find everything under My Bookings. Your own sessions show in blue with the door code. Tap ✏️ Modify to change the lane, machine, time or length, or Cancel to cancel.

  • Costs more? (e.g. longer, or Truman) — you pay just the difference (your credit is used first).
  • Costs less? (e.g. shorter) — the difference comes back as account credit.
  • Cancel at least the cutoff time before you start (usually 2 hours) — you get the value back as credit. Closer to the start it locks (🔒).
  • Within 2 hours of your start you can still move up to 1 hour earlier or extend (paying the difference).

4. Joining a waitlist

If an hour is fully booked it shows JOIN WAITLIST. Tap it, pick the times you want, and we'll email you the moment any lane opens at those times (from a cancellation or change). It's first-in, first-refusal — you get an exclusive hold to grab it before the next person.

6. Family & kids (My Athletes)

Under Profile → My Athletes you can add your children as athletes and pick their coach(es). When a coach books a session for your child, you get the details and a door code — tap 📱 SMS Details on their session to text the code and time straight to a phone.

One account can hold the whole family. Add each child, choose their coach, and their coaching sessions appear under your bookings with their own access code.

7. Payments & account credit

Card payments are handled securely by Stripe. Any time a booking is cancelled or shortened, the value comes back as account credit — it's applied automatically at your next checkout. See it all under Payments & Credit, including receipts/tax invoices.

8. Notifications you'll get

If you install the app and allow notifications, you'll also get push notifications on your phone — the door code comes first so you can get straight in.

Push notifications

  • Booking confirmed — with your door code
  • Session reminder — shortly before you start (door code first)
  • A waitlist slot opened — tap Accept to grab it, or pass to the next person
  • Booking changed or cancelled
  • Account credit added
  • A mate added you to a booking

You choose which push categories you want and can turn them off per device (under Profile → Notifications). Email still covers everything below.

9. Add the app to your phone

Install Cricket Revolution to your home screen for one-tap booking and push notifications.

  • iPhone (Safari): tap the Share button (square with an arrow) → Add to Home Screen → Add.
  • Android (Chrome): open the menu and choose Install app / Add to Home screen, or tap Install now when prompted.
